About Us

For Code First Girls, we wanted to base our project around a topic we were all passionate about and had in common; which was video games! We also all had experience with art and illustration, so depicting video games as drinks seemed like a really eye-catching way of introducing people to new games they may not have played before.

We hope people will discover a new game or drink they would like to try!
