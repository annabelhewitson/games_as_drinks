Peach & Orange Smoothie

Fresh & Fruity

Orange juice, frozen peaches, peach yogurt and a bit of honey blend to peach-orange smoothie perfection in this recipe.

Have a refreshing glass in the summer whilst catching butterflies or relaxing on the beach.

Representing the fresh and sweet nature of Animal Crossing: New Horizons, this smoothie uses the popular fruit that is common in the game. Each town starts with one of five types of fruit as its native fruit: Apples, Oranges, Cherries, Pears, or Peaches which can be eaten, given as gifts to other villagers, or sold.

Animal Crossing: New Horizons is a 2020 life simulation game developed and published by Nintendo known for its relaxing and casual gameplay.

Illustrated by Annie Hathaway
